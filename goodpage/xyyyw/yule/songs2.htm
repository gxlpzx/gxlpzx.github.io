Comments on the song!

Have you ever watched the movie "Casablanca"? This movie won the Oscar Award in 1943. The old sad love story in it are still remembered. About 30 years later, "Casablanca" appeared in another movie about a couple in love while watching "Casablanca". In this movie Bertie Higgins sang: "I guess there are many broken hearts in Casablanca. You know I've never really been there. So I don't know..." Bertie' s deep voice expressed a gloomy mood and this song became so popular that nearly everyone knew it. Even now years after this song was released, it is still loved by many people. If you are a music fan, you really can't miss it!
