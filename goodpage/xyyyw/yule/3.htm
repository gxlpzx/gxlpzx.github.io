1. 租房

A large family, with seven children, moved to a new
city. They were having a difficult time finding an
apartment to live in. Many apartments were large enough,
but the landlords objected to the large family. After
several days of searching, the father asked the mother
to take the four younger children to visit the cemetery,
while he took the older three to find an apartment.

After they had looked most of the morning they found a
place that was just right. Then the landlord asked the
usual question: "How many children do you have?" The
father answered with a deep sigh, "Seven...but four are
with their dear mother in the cemetery." He got the
apartment.!
