4.名气是什么？

Three executives were sitting around trying to put a
definition on the word "fame." One said: "Fame is
being invited to the White House for a talk with the
President."

The second executive said: "No, fame is being invited to
the White House for a talk with the President-and when
the Hot Line interrupts the conversation, he doesn't
answer it."

The third executive said, "You're both wrong. Fame is
being invited to the White House for a talk with the
President and when the Hot Line rings, he answers it,
listens a moment, and then says..."here, it's for you."
