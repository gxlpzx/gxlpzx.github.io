2.学聪明

Three men are about to be executed. One's an American, one's a
German and one's a stupid person. The guard brings the American
forward and the executioner asks if he has any last requests.
He says no and the executioner shouts, "Ready!...Aim!!..."
Suddenly the American yells, "EARTHQUAKE!!!" Everyone
is startled and looks all around. He escapes.
The guard brings the German forward and the executioner asks if he
has any last requests. He says no and the executioner shouts,
"Ready!...Aim!!..." Suddenly the German yells, 'TORNADO!!!"
Again everyone is startled and looks around. He escapes.
By now the stupid person has it all figured out. The guard brings him forward
and the executioner asks if he has any last requests. He says no
and the executioner shouts, "Ready!...Aim!!..."
...and the stupid person yells, "FIRE!!!"
