• “Journey to the West” Stir up Youngster 需要插件播放听朗读

    Hong Kong-made film “Journey to the West” hits the taste of more and more Chinese youth, especially the college students. Quoting the “classical” dialogue in the film becomes a common practice of their daily life.

    The film is considered “a wild adaptation” of the ancient Chinese classic novel written by Wu Chen’en. Starred by Steven Chow (Zhou Xinchi), a well-known Hong Kong comedian, the film is designed as a post-modern, tragic-comedy. Amused by the funny language, the “Journey” fans are also touched by the love story of the Monkey King.

    According to the 21st Century, Qinghua University is regarded as the place for the film’s revival. The film’s initial box-office record was poor. On average a Qinghua student may have seen this film for at least four times. Its funny lines become part of their daily language.

    Qinghua’s famous BBS pushed on the trend. An increasing number of homepages and websites of “Journey” or “Steven Chow” boomed up after the film became known throughout the country. The film is generally regarded as a classic of the so-called X-generation in China.
