Unit 15 第十五单元

Could you answer the telephone, please?

  Could you answer the telephone, please? 你能接一下电话吗？Could you …? 表示说话人的一种委婉请求或要求。Answer the telephone接电话。Answer还有“回答”的意思。例如：

  Could you carry the books to the classroom, please?

  你能把书带到教室里去吗？

  Could I open the door?

  我能打开窗户吗？

  My sister answered the telephone.

  我妹妹接的电话。

  I'm sorry I can't answer your questions?

  对不起，我回答不了你的问题。


I'll get it?

I'll get it? 我来接电话。这是一句最常用的接电话的句子。当电话铃响的时候，表示自己来接电话。

Could you ask him to call me, please?

  Could you ask him to call me, please? 你能叫他给我打个电话吗？call me 表示“给我打电话” ，也可以说成call me up。类似打电话的词还有telephone, phone和ring。例如：

  I want to telephone my sister.

  我想给我妹妹打个电话。

  Who phoned you yesterday?

  昨天谁给你打电话？

  Please ring me up at six o'clock tomorrow morning.

  请明天早上六点钟给我打个电话。

Did you write it down?

Did you write it down? 你把它（电话号码）记下来了吗？write…down 表示“把…写（记）下来” 。后面可跟介词短语on… 。例如：

  Please write these words on your exercise books.

  请把这些词写在练习薄上。

  Would you like to write your name down on my book，please?

  请把你的名词写在我的书上，好吗？

There were many apples on the trees.

  There were many apples on the trees. 树上有好多苹果。There were ….表示存在的句型，是 there be….的过去式。例如：

  There were many trees in our school.

  我们学校曾经有许多树。

  Was there a car before our classroom yesterday?

  昨天我们教室的前面有一辆汽车吗？
