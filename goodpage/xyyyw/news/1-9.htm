Chinese Enthusiastic about 2008 Games Bid

With the Olympics of the year 2000 being held, Sydney is the focus of the world’ attention. Meanwhile, Chinese are enthusiastic about 2008 Games bid.

The International Olympic Committee (IOC) announced the candidates of 2008 Games this summer. Beijing is one the list with its opponents being Paris, Toronto, Osaka, and Istanbul.

To become the host of 2008 Summer Olympic Games is the wish of every citizen of Beijing. Wherever you go in the city, the slogan " New Beijing, Great Olympics " will catch your eye. It shows up the public aspiration.

"Most of the taxis in Beijing are designed for Asian people, and I'm afraid that tall foreign friends may feel uncomfortable sitting in the cars if the 2008 Olympics are held here," said Wang Weidong, a taxi driver. What he said expresses the strong feeling among the citizens. Wang also said, " To host the games will undoubtedly benefit Beijing and China greatly, but I believe to get the ultimate win, we need to join our efforts."

Beijing has devised a plan to make the city greener, which is only one of the measures to be taken. The plan covers improvement of air quality and water quality. Everyone will be motivated to work for a better Beijing, an official said.

And the city will also update sports facilities, such as building a Colossal Olympic Center. Advanced TV broadcast systems will also be available.

On July 15 2001, during its 112th session in Moscow, the IOC will vote for the host city of 2008 Games.
