Ⅸ阅读理解（１０）

    The earth moves round the sun,and the moon moves round the earth.When our part （部分） of the earth turns to the sun,it is day,When our part of the earth turns away from the sun,it is night.

    The sun is much bigger than the moon.But sometimes the moon looks bigger than the sun,because （因为）it's much nearer to the earth.

    The sun is very bright（明亮）.It gives a very strong light（光）.The moon looks quite bright,too.But it doesn't give any light at all.

    The moon looks much bigger and brighter than the stars（星）.But acutally （事实上）the stars are much bigger and brighterthan the moon.They look smaller than the moon because they're much factier away from us.
