Aesop(伊索) and the Traveller(旅行者)

Long, long ago there lived a clever man. His name was Aesop. One
day, as he was taking a walk, he met a traveller. The traveller
stopped him and asked:"kind man, could you tell me how soon I'll get
to town?"

"Go," Aesop answered.

"I know I must go,"said the traveller,"but I want to know how soon
I'll get to town?"

"Go," Aesop said again angrily.

"This man must be mad(疯)." the traveller thought and went on.

After the traveller had gone some distance (距离), Aesop shouted
after him.

"You'll get to town in two hours." He turned back and asked "Why
didn't you tell me that before?"

"How could I have told you that before?" Aesop answered, "I didn't
know how fast you could walk."
