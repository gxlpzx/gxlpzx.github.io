Dear Ding Qianlu:

I hope you are well. Thank you for you last letter:

You asked me the weather here in Nan Chang. There are four seasons in the year here:spring,summer,autumn and winter.

Spring is better season of the year: The days get longer; and the nights get shorter: In spring, everything begins to grow. The air is fine and clean.

Summer is the hottest season of the year. It often rains. But I like it very much. Because is good for sports. I like swimming. I am good at swimming.

Autumn is a busy season. On the farm, all the farmers are very busy. Because is harvest time. And we must help them with the harvest. This year we are going to help them pick apples.

Winter is the coldest season of the year. The days are very shorter, and the nights are very longer. Sometimes it snows. Some people do not like it. But I like it very much. Because it good for go skating. All the framers are busy. Too. Getting ready for the next year.

Yours, Xu Yubing.
